About Us

Who we are & What we do

Hello! We are Simpacc!

Accounting, bookkeeping, payroll & tax services based in Halifax, NS

Two friends, Arnel and Karl, started Simpacc to make life easier for their friends (their first clients) to focus on their businesses. Since our founding in late 2018, we have worked with hundreds of repeat clients and still growing…

At Simpacc, we empower our clients to make educated choices about their businesses, one transaction at a time. We understand that small and medium-sized businesses depend on their financial stability and growth to remain competitive in the current business climate. We fully understand the challenges facing businesses today and will find the creative solutions necessary to grow your business.

We partner with our clients to propel their businesses to success through thoughtful and honest communication, knowledge transfer, and strategically employing technology, whenever possible. We are confident that by engaging you in the process, you will better understand your finances and how to run your company more efficiently and effectively.

With more then 30 years of combined financial management and accounting experience, we are ready to get started on the road map to your business’ success!
